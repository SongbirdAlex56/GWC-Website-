Hello and Welcome!

Thank you for visiting this website. This website is a place for all people to learn and reflect about the disparity black women face in STEM fields. This is a big problem for everyone because we want STEM fields to reflect and involve all sorts of people especially when those same fields claim to better society at large. I hope that after spending time on this website, you will be able to see the importance of hving more black women in STEM fields and help make a change.
