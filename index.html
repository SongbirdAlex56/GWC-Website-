Fight for More Representation in STEM!

STEM fields are great and rewarding careers. More black women need to be seen and heard in them

Why does this Matter?

The people working in STEM fields need to represent society as a whole. STEM is here to build a more diverse, accessible future. Black women need to become apart of this. In the 2017-2018 school year, black women earned only 2.9% of Bachelor's degrees across all STEM fields.
